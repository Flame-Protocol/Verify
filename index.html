Paymaster Settlement Model

Understanding why no market purchase occurs. Blockfills operates as paymaster, not buyer.

KEY DISTINCTION

Retail USDT: Market purchase → Price discovery → Order book → Fees/spreads
Paymaster USDT: Settlement acceptance → No price discovery → Prefunded capacity → Direct wire
PAYMASTER ROLE
Settlement intermediary - NOT a buyer. Facilitates conversion without market exposure.
NO MARKET EVENT
No order books, no bids, no price discovery. Token treated as settlement instrument.
PREFUNDED CAPACITY
Blockfills maintains prefunded fiat, credit lines, or inventory agreements for instant conversion.
INTERNAL NETTING
Token delivered → Obligation extinguished internally → Fiat released via banking rails.
